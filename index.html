The very mention of soft – or cool – drinks brings back to mind Goli Soda.

Time was when Goli Soda was served to guests at home as a token of honour.

In 1767, a cool beverage aerated with carbon dioxide was manufactured for the first time in the world. Joseph Priestley invented the carbonation process.

Today, Relish gives a new lease of life to Goli Soda, with carefully chosen natural ingredients, keeping in mind the health benefits and bringing out exotic flavors of the sub-continent.
